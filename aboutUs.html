Meet the CEO:

P. Kac is the CEO, founder, and namesake of PK Industries. When not designing a circuit board or project, P. Kac enjoys reading, pixel art, and photography. He has served as the Team Captain of Build on FTC Robotics Team 22064 for 3 years, and Build Team Lead on FRC Robotics Team 167. P. Kac taught himself C++ code, microcontroller embedded programming, and circuit board design. He has also taken classes in JavaScript, Java, Fusion 360, and Introductory Electronics.
